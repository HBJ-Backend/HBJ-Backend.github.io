About

MindCraft Alpha

Explore the world of Computer Science in just 1 day! On the day of the MindCraft workshop, students will have the opportunity to explore CS in two different sessions, robotics and computational thinking. Both sessions are delivered in an interactive manner, where students get to learn, apply, play, and enjoy with their friends. The goal of these workshops is to spread awarness about the field of CS & its importance. To each workshop we invite 80 to 100 students from high schools all around Qatar. To make our workshops more relatable & applicable, we offer different themes like sustainability & cybersecurity. Read more about what we do for each theme below!

Robotics

Robotics is essential for teaching computer science, offering fun, interactive, and easy exploration for students. In this workshop, students learn basic programming concepts and program robots to solve various challenges, with themes changing annually to include topics like self-driving cars and AI.

Computational Thinking

Computer scientists need skills beyond coding, such as problem solving and critical thinking. At MindCraft, students explore various aspects of computer science, including computational thinking topics like searching, sorting, cryptography, and algorithms like the traveling salesman problem.

Sustainability

In our Robotics session, we engage students with a set of challenges revolving around Sustainability. Students program a robot to automate tasks around the farm, saving both time & energy and helping humans manage a sustainable environment.

  • Program the robot to clean solar panels
  • Make Cozmo deliver items around a farm
  • Learn new ways to automate our tasks and help save the planet!

Sports

Students in our Robotics session face exciting challenges, where they program Cozmo robots to play football. From scoring goals to passing to teammates, these tasks encourage collaboration and sharpen their programming skills. Using if-else statments & loops, students will be able to program a robot to check for a player & pass a ball to them.

  • Program the robot to pass to a player
  • Make Cozmo score a goal!
  • Learn new ways to control a robot

Cybersecurity

During Computational Thinking, we cover algorithms in cryptography along with many famous algorithms in computer science. We teach students how to enhance their problem solving skills in the field of computer science.

  • Learn different kinds of cryptographic algorithms
  • Solve a series of puzzles to get to the treasure
  • Aquire the skills necessary to be a computer scientist

Navigation

In our Computational Thinking session, we explore algorithms that help us navigate more efficiently. Students will discover how Google Maps determines the shortest routes and how to find the most efficient way to visit multiple countries in one trip.

  • Learn how to find the shortest path!
  • Solve a series of puzzles to get to the treasure
  • Aquire the skills necessary to be a computer scientist

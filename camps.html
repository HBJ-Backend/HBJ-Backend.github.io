Mindcraft Camps

Explore cutting-edge topics in Computer Science! Looking for a hands-on opportunity to gain real-world skills? MindCraft camps offer an immersive experience where participants dive deep into a computer science topic, acquire specific skills, and apply their knowledge to real-life scenarios. Our camps are project-driven, allowing students to create fully functioning projects, whether it's a simple tic-tac-toe game or a complex traffic sign detection system. There's a great sense of accomplishment when students see their own creations solving real challenges. Learn more about the exciting projects students develop at each camp below.

  • Practice concepts by developing you own solutions to a given set of problems.
  • Build your own project and get the opportunity to put together everything you learnt.
  • 18 hours conducted over one week or several weeks.
  • Catering to different age groups & offering various fun activitie.s

Programming

Who said learning how to code is boring or difficult? In our camps we take the participants in a rich hands-on experience where they learn how to program and build their own project. The camps allow participants to learn the syntax and context of the language using the simplest and most effective methods. We don’t stop at teaching the concept, participants will also reflect everything they learn on day-to-day applications. We currently have two camps:

Camp 1

  • Name: Intro to Python
  • Project: Tic-Tac-Toe

Camp 2

  • Name: Intermediate Python
  • Project: Traffic Violation System

Computational Thinking

Designed for anyone with an interest to learn programming but doesn’t know where to start. We use an interactive tool that simplifies text-based programming allowing participants to grasp the concepts without worrying about the syntax. Participant apply all what they learn on real life scenarios such as vacuum cleaners and warehouse management.

Camp 1

  • Name: RoboMind Beginners:
  • Project: Warehouse Management

Camp 2

  • Name: RoboMind Intermediate
  • Project: Search and Rescue

Cybersecurity Camp

Dive into the world of cybersecurity with our hands-on camp designed for aspiring digital defenders. Learn the fundamentals of cryptography and master command line tools to uncover hidden digital flags. This camp offers an exciting blend of theory and practice, empowering students to safeguard data and navigate the complexities of the cyber world. Perfect for those eager to explore the techniques used by security professionals, this camp is your gateway to a future in cybersecurity.

Camp 1

  • Name: Cybersecurity Camp
  • Project: CTF Challenges

Artificial Intelligence

Embark on an exciting journey into the world of Artificial Intelligence with our immersive AI camps. Designed for curious minds, this camp introduces students to the core concepts of AI, including machine learning, neural networks, and data analysis. Through interactive projects and hands-on activities, participants will learn how to create intelligent systems that can solve real-world problems.

Camp 1

  • Name: AI:Vision
  • Project: Traffic Sign Detection

Camp 2

  • Name: AI:Language
  • Project: Sentiment Analysis
